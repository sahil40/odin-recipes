Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken biryani is a flavorful, aromatic rice dish made by layering spiced chicken and basmati rice, then cooking them together so all the flavors blend.

Ingredients

  • 300g chicken
  • 1 cup yogurt (curd)
  • Salt
  • Turmeric (½ tsp)
  • Garam masala (1 tsp)

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil
  2. Add chopped onion + garlic → cook till soft
  3. Add ground chicken (or any meat)
  4. Cook until browned
  5. Add tomato puree + salt + spices
