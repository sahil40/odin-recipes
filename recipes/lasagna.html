Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic baked Italian dish made by layering wide, flat pasta sheets with rich fillings, then baking it until bubbling and golden.

Ingredients

  • Ground chicken / beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil
  2. Add chopped onion + garlic → cook till soft
  3. Add ground chicken (or any meat)
  4. Cook until browned
  5. Add tomato puree + salt + spices
